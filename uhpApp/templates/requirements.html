To fulfill the Honors Event Requirements, students are required to attend at least 1 UHP Event and 1 Social Justice Event

UHP Event

Designed to build community among Honors students as well as give Honors students a break from their day to day academic activities

Can be completed by:
  • Attending events sponsored by UHP
  • Becoming a UHP Mentor
  • Attending the Honors peer advising
  • Attending the Honors Thesis Poster Session
  • Attending any event advertised as a UHP Event in the weekly Honors Program newsletter

Social Justice Event

Designed to encourage Honors students to expand their perspectives beyond the classroom by participating in an on-campus event focused on a social justice issue.

Can be completed by:
  • Volunteering with SCCAP
  • Attending events on campus advertised as Honors Social Justice Events
  • Speak with the HAC Social Justice chair for other opportunities or to get permission to attend a non-advertised event for Social Justice credit
